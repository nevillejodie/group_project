B.Hub: Relax

Need a time out? Birmingham is the place to go. From beautiful Birmingham Botanical Gardens to cultural hub Birmingham Museum and Art Gallery, Brum has something for everyone.


Founded in 1829, the botanical gardens boast planet species from around the world. A social hub for the city of Birmingham, the gardens are often the hosts of company parties, political meetings, and music and drama festivals. Go for a relaxing walk to the sounds of water and birds, right in the heart of the city.



Recently completed Green Heart at the University of Birmingham boasts 12 acres of green space, with grass ampitheatre and energy generating pavements: the Green Heart is an eco space with a friendly student atmosphere.


Historical house with its own botanical gardens, Winterbourne offers a unique cultural experience. Explore the house then enjoy a tranquil scroll through the gardens
