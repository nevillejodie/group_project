B-Hub: Play

Explore the array of venues and events that Birmingham has to offer. From art spaces to streetfood, music gigs to musuems. Here are some of our top reccomendations of places to visit when you're ready to Play.

Ikon Gallery
Internationally acclaimed contemporary art venue located in Brindleyplace, Ikon Gallery hosts regular exhibitions, workshops and events with free entry.


Digbeth Dining Club
Voted the Best Street Food Event in the UK (2014), DDC hosts locally sourced streetfood on Lower Trinity St every Thurs-Sun.


Custard Factory
The heart of Birmingham's creative and digital district. Custard Factory is a hub home to many independent shops, cafes, bars and restaurants.
