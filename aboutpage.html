About B.Hub

This website lists spaces in Birmingham by category. We hope this will make it easier to find out where to go based on your mood or needs. We also hope that this website encourages you to find balance between working, playing and relaxing.
This website was created as part of the curriculum for the CodeFirst:Girls Beginners coding course. CodeFirst:Girls aims to get more women into tech by offering them subsidised teaching at a range of locations across the United Kingdom.

The course was hosted and delivered by the awesome developers at The Economist in Birmingham.
