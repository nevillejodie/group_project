City Centre

Explore the work-friendly spaces that Birmingham has to offer. From coffee shops to libraries, bakerys to learning centres. Here are some of our top recommendations for places to work.


Named so because they roast their coffee at a slightly lower temperature, 200 Degrees is perfect if you need a little sustenance while you work. Boasting a range of delicious coffee and cake along with its cosy interior, this is the place to go if you are more productive in an atmospheric environment.
